About Me

I am an associate professor at the School of Automation, Southeast University (SEU). Before joining SEU, I received a doctorate of science from Lanzhou University, Lanzhou, China, and a doctorate of engineering from Waseda University, Tokyo, Japan. My research focuses on computer vision, with a special interest in multi-view stereo.

I am looking for doctoral/master students who are genuinely passionate about research in computer vision.

Research Interests

  • Computer Vision Algorithms: image matching/registration, multi-view stereo, 3D human behavior analysis
  • High-Speed Vision Systems: highly-parallel video processing, hardware-friendly machine learning
